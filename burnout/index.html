Burnout!

Burnouts are real. For I know what it feels like and what it does to your mind and body. Now what I am about to write here is purely based on my personal experience. None of the material has been any kind of research so take it with a pinch of salt.

What burnout feels like?

It feels like you not having any energy or enthusiasm to deal with what is going on anymore. Energy in the body and enthusiasm in your head!
